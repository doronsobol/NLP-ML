Young was one of the performers at the BBC's "Live at Blackpool" programme on BBC Radio 2 on Saturday 5 September 2009.
www.bbc.co.uk/radio2/events/live-in-blackpool/2009/artists/will-young/
William Robert "Will" Young (born on 20 January 1979) is a multi-award winning singer-songwriter and actor from the United Kingdom. He rose to fame in 2002 after winning the inaugural Pop Idol contest, making him the world's first winner of the now-worldwide Idols-format franchise.
