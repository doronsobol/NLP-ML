In England, the 1st Battalion Irish Guards marched in a St Patrick"s Day parade held at the Victoria Barracks in Windsor. In Auckland, New Zealand, these drinkers got into the festive spirit. In New York, these competitive eaters skipped the traditional pints of stout for corned beef and rye sandwiches. Winner Pat Bertoletti (left) consumed 16 3/4 sandwiches in ten minutes to win the contest. The fountain in front of the White House flowed with green water to mark the day.
