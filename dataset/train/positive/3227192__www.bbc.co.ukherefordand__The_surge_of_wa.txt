More than 50 people were rescued by Hereford and Worcester Fire and Rescue Service over the weekend of Saturday 6 and Sunday 7 September, as they were kept busy by the extensive flooding across the two counties of Herefordshire and Worcestershire.  One of the area's worst hit was North Worcestershire.
