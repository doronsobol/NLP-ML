The current crest, featuring a ceremonial lion rampant regardant holding a staff, is a modification of the one introduced in the early 1950s.

The club have chosen a design similar to their 1955 badge, when they won their only league title.  Chief executive Peter Kenyon said: "As the club embarks on a new and very exciting era, we have a new identity that reflects our tradition.  "We are incredibly proud of Chelsea's heritage. It was a conscious decision to base this badge on the 1950s one."
