Labour MP Tom Watson has compared News International chairman James Murdoch to a 'mafia boss,' during a parliamentary session about phone hacking at the News of the World newspaper.  Mr Watson has been a leading figure in the investigation into the extent of phone hacking and the degree to which senior company figures, including Mr Murdoch knew what was going on.
