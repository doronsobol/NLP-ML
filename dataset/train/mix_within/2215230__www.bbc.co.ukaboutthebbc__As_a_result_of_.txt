The BBC’s Executive Committee is responsible for the operational management of the Corporation. Chaired by the Director-General it consists of nine directors who manage large operational areas of the BBC.  The Executive Committee is responsible for delivering the BBC’s services in accordance with the strategy and delegation framework agreed by the Board.  This page was last updated on 30 June 2017.
