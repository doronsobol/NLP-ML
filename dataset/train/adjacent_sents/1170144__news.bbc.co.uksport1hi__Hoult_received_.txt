Gary Jones scored the winning spot-kick to help Rochdale beat Stoke 4-2 on penalties in the Carling Cup after both teams shared four goals in 120 minutes.  Ryan Shawcross gave Stoke a fourth minute lead and they looked good for the win before David Perkins equalised late in the game.  Richard Cresswell pounced to send the tie to penalties that saw Stoke miss two before Jones fired in the winner.
