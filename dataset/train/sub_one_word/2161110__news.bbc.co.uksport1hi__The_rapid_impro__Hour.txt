The Hornets won four of their five league games - beating Sheffield United, Coventry, Brighton and Cardiff, although they were defeated by Leeds.  They are now in third place in the table, chasing Sheffield United for the second automatic promotion spot.  The other contenders were Iain Dowie of Crystal Palace, Ipswich manager Joe Royle and Leeds boss Kevin Blackwell.
