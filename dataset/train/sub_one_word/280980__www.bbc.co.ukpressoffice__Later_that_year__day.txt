Mike Stock began writing songs at the age of seven and taught himself to play guitar and piano, signing a publishing contract whilst still a teenager.  Teaming up with Matt Aitken in 1982, and subsequently with Pete Waterman in 1984, Mike Stock is a founder member and songwriter, musician and producer of the Stock Aitken Waterman team which was so successful in the popular music world in the Eighties and Nineties.  Mike has succeeded with over 300 hit singles, including 16 No. 1s with a record 11 different artists in the UK alone.  Songs and lyrics of his continue to feature in award-winning films, stage theatrical presentations and on TV and radio.
