Where and when was it?  Faithless, Chemical Brothers, Deus, The Offspring, The Streets, Groove Armada, Kings of Leon, Hundred Reasons.  Pukkelpop is one of the most determinedly "alternative" festivals in Europe, being run by a group called the Humanistic Youth of Leopoldsburg. It aims to be a "progressive and contemporary" musical event.  Just six bands played at the festival's launch in 1985 - now over 100 appear.  Where can I find out more?
